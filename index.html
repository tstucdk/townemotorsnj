Arnold Brauer founded Towne Motors with a vision to bring exceptional vehicles and customer service to New Jersey drivers. What started as a single dealership quickly grew, built on trust, care, and a commitment to community. Today, that legacy continues into the next generation—still grounded in the same core values. Our Toyota dealer in New Jersey has been family owned and operated since 1976, but our history goes back even further to 1962 when Arnold Brauer opened his first Ford dealership in Madison, NJ. Arnold spent his youth helping out at his father’s Graham-Paige dealership, so it isn’t tough to see that we know a thing or two about building relationships and fostering loyalty. No matter what your needs are, we're here to help you find exactly what you're after at Towne Toyota, Towne Hyundai-Denville and the new Towne Hyundai-Hackettstown.
